🌟 Perfect Draft Guide 🌟

⚠️ Disclaimer: Only players from the S53 PBE Cards Binder are guaranteed inclusion to Tournament.

🏆 Welcome to the Perfect Draft! 🏆

🔹 Emulating OOTP's Perfect Team, this draft lets you build your dream roster in 12 exciting rounds!

🔹 If you are collecting cards, you will get to keep all 20 cards you select!

  • Keep that in mind when you are drafting! Do you want to focus on filling your collection or winning the tournament?

🃏 Draft Overview:

🔹 12 rounds to choose your cards

🔹 Everyone will choose from the same 6 cards in Rounds 1-2

  • Starting in Round 3, there will be more than 6 cards available, and each user will get to pick from a random 6.
    • The exact numbers will be shown above the cards as "displaying 6 of ___ cards."
    • Above that is a colored box displaying what types of cards you pick from this round.

📊 Draft Requirements:

🔹 You must select between 9-14 hitters

🔹 You must select between 7-11 pitchers

🔹 Exceeding limits will trigger an "All draft slots are full!" notification

🖱️ How to Draft:

🔹 You can Click on cards to view stats

🔹 Hit the "Draft" button to make your selection

🔹 Watch as the Draft History updates with your picks

📌 Key Information:

🔹 Round number, available cards, and picks left are displayed above

🔹 Colored notifications matching card borders, indicate card types

🔹 You may not go back once a pick is made
